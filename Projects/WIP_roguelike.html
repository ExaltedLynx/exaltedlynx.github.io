Ransacker

WIP Dungeon Crawler + Inventory Management Game

Ransacker is a real time inventory management & dungeon crawling roguelike. Adventure through various dungeons to collect as much valuable loot as you can possibly carry while also defending yourself, with the loot you find, from tougher and tougher monsters without escaping empty handed.

I worked on the following:
  • Designed the main mechanics and systems for the gameplay loop.
  • Designed the attacks and mechanics for each enemy
  • Implemented an inventory system along with a "hotbar" where the player places the items they want to use.
  • item system with stats
  • scaling difficulty over time
  • Loot tables
  • Enemy weakpoint system & item effect system
  • Procedurally generated grid based map for the dungeon floors.
